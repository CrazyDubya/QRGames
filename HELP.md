## Playing Trivia

### Game Flow

1. **Questions are displayed one at a time** with four answer options
2. **Select your answer** by clicking on one of the options
3. **Submit your answer** before time runs out (if timer is enabled)
4. **See if you're correct** - correct answers earn points
5. **Move to the next question** when the host advances
6. **Final results** show player rankings by score

### How to Play

- **Read the question carefully** at the top of the screen
- **Click on your chosen answer** from the four options
- The selected option will be highlighted in purple
- **Click "Submit Answer"** to lock in your choice
- Wait for feedback showing if you were correct
- The correct answer will be displayed in green
- Incorrect selections will be shown in red

### Scoring

- Each correct answer earns **1 point**
- The player with the most points at the end wins
- Scores are displayed on the final leaderboard

### Tips

- Answer quickly for competitive play
- Pay attention to the question number to track progress
- The host controls when to move to the next question

## Playing Bingo

### Game Flow

1. **Receive your unique bingo card** with random numbers
2. **Numbers are called one at a time** by the host
3. **Mark numbers** on your card as they're called
4. **Choose a winning pattern** (single line, 4 corners, or full card)
5. **Click "BINGO!"** when you complete the pattern
6. **First player to complete the pattern wins!**

### Bingo Card

Each bingo card has:
- **5 columns** labeled B-I-N-G-O
- **5 rows** of numbers
- A **FREE space** in the center (always marked)
- Numbers range from 1-75, distributed across columns:
  - B: 1-15
  - I: 16-30
  - N: 31-45
  - G: 46-60
  - O: 61-75

### Winning Patterns

Choose from three winning patterns:

#### 1. Single Line
Complete any:
- **Horizontal row** (5 in a row)
- **Vertical column** (5 in a column)
- **Diagonal** (corner to corner)

#### 2. Four Corners
Mark all four corners of your card:
- Top-left
- Top-right
- Bottom-left
- Bottom-right

#### 3. Full Card
Mark **all 25 spaces** on your card (including the FREE space)

### How to Play

1. **Watch for called numbers** displayed at the top
2. **Click on numbers** on your card to mark them
   - You can only mark numbers that have been called
   - Marked numbers turn green
3. **Select your winning pattern** using the buttons at the bottom
4. **Click "BINGO!"** when you complete the pattern
5. If valid, you win! If not, keep playing

### Called Numbers

- The **current number** is displayed in large text at the top
- **All called numbers** are shown as chips below
- The **total count** of called numbers is displayed
- Numbers are called in random order

### Tips

- Pay close attention when numbers are called
- Mark your numbers quickly
- You can only mark numbers that have been called
- Choose an easier pattern (4 corners) for a quicker game
- Choose full card for a longer, more challenging game

## Technical Requirements

- **For Hosts**: Desktop or laptop with a modern web browser
- **For Players**: Smartphone, tablet, or computer with camera (for QR scanning)
- **Internet Connection**: Required for all participants
- **Supported Browsers**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Scan QR Code
- Ensure your camera permissions are enabled
- Try entering the join URL manually
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection

### Players Not Appearing
- Check that all players are on the same lobby code
- Refresh the page to reconnect
- Ensure the server is running properly

### Game Not Starting
- Make sure at least one player has joined
- Only the host can start the game
- Try refreshing and rejoining if there are connection issues
